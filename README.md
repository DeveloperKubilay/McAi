## What is this?
Ever wanted a Minecraft friend who never logs off, always has your back, and can do *literally* anything you say? Meet your new Minecraft AI! 🧑‍💻🎮

## Why tho? 🤔
Have you ever wished you had one more friend while playing Minecraft? That’s exactly what this project is for! Your bot hangs out with you, chats, manages your items, farms, fishes, and does so much more. The goal is to make Minecraft more fun and dynamic by giving you a smart companion who’s always by your side. In short, you’re never alone in the game!

## How to Use
1. Clone this repo (duh)
2. Install dependencies: `npm install`
3. **If you want to use Gemini or Azure AI features, make sure to add your API tokens to `config.js` or a `.env` file!**
   - Example for `.env`:
     ```env
     GEMINI_API_KEY=your_gemini_token_here
     AZURE_API_KEY=your_azure_token_here
     ```
   - Or edit `config.js` and add your keys as needed.
4. Run a bot: `node index.js`

## Example Commands
- `come` – Bot comes to you
- `follow` – Bot follows you everywhere (clingy much?)
- `fire` – Shoots nearest mob
- `craft 10 bread` – Crafts 10 bread (if you have the stuff)
- `give apple` – Gives you an apple (if it has one)
- `sleep` – Bot goes to bed (good night, king)
